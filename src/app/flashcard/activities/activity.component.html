Activities Flashcard


Parents should show the activity flashcard to the child and make the sound in front of them. We should encourage kids to mimic the sound and recognize the activity.

Parents should let child recognize the activities before telling them. Sometime, they may recognize incorrectly but with regular practise they will improve.

Continuous practise of the flashcard may help to develop speech or improve speech in children.

Activity Flashcards

Activities
  • Age: 1 to 4 years
  • Description: Common kids activities
  • Flashcard words: Brush my hair, Brush my teeth, Comb my hair, Eat breakfast, Eat dinner, Exercise, Floss, Get dressed, Go to Bed, Go to school, Make breakfast, Make lunch, Play game, Play Instrument, Pay soccer
