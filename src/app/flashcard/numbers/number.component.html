Number Flashcard


Numbers are important, once kids recognise the numbers we should encourage to count. Parents should show the counting flashcard to the child and ask them to count the images with fingers.

Parents should let child count the objects in the flashcard before telling them. Sometime, they may count incorrectly but with regular practise they will improve.

Continuous practise of the flashcard may help to develop or improve brain capabilities in children.
