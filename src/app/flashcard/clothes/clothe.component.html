Clothes Flashcard


Parents should show the clothes flashcard to the child and make the sound in front of them. We should encourage kids to mimic the sound and recognize the clothe.

Parents should let child recognize the clothes before telling them. Sometime, they may recognize incorrectly but with regular practise they will improve.

Continuous practise of the flashcard may help to develop speech or improve speech in children.
