Shapes Flashcard


Parents should show the shape flashcard to the child and make the sound in front of them. We should encourage kids to mimic the sound and recognize the animal.

Parents should let child recognize the shapes before telling them. Sometime, they may recognize incorrectly but with regular practise they will improve.

Continuous practise of the flashcard may help to develop speech or improve speech in children.

Shape Flashcards

Shapes
  • Age: 1 to 4 years
  • Description: Common shapes kids should know
  • Flashcard words: Arrow, Circle, Cross, Diamond, Heart, Hexagon, Moon, Octagon, Oval, Parallelogram, Pentagon, Rectangle, Square, Star, Trapezoid, Triangle
