Appliances Flashcard


We are surrounded with home appliances. Parents should show the home appliance flashcard to the child and make the sound in front of them. We should encourage kids to mimic the sound and recognize the home appliances.

Parents should let child recognize the home appliances before telling them. Sometime, they may recognize incorrectly but with regular practise they will improve.

Continuous practise of the flashcard may help to develop speech or improve speech in children.

Appliance Flashcards

Appliances
  • Age: 1 to 4 years
  • Description: Common Appliances around us
  • Flashcard words: Air condition, Blender, Dishwasher, Fan, Food processor, Fridge, Gas stove, Hairdryer, Iron, Juicer, Microwave, Mixer, Sandwich Maker, Toaster, Vacuum Cleaner, Washing Machine
