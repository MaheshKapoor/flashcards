Professions Flashcard


Parents should show the Profession flashcard to the child and make the sound in front of them. We should encourage kids to mimic the sound and recognize the Profession.

Parents should let child recognize the Professions before telling them. Sometime, they may recognize incorrectly but with regular practise they will improve.

Continuous practise of the flashcard may help to develop speech or improve speech in children.

Profession Flashcards

Professions
  • Age: 1 to 4 years
  • Description: Common Professions of people
  • Flashcard words: Artist, Astronaut, Bus driver, Chef, Dentist, Doctor, Farmer, Fire Fighter, Fisherman
