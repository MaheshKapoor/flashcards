Colors Flashcard


Parents should show the color flashcard to the child and make the sound in front of them. We should encourage kids to mimic the sound and recognize the animal.

Parents should let child recognize the colors before telling them. Sometime, they may recognize incorrectly but with regular practise they will improve.

Continuous practise of the flashcard may help to develop speech or improve speech in children.

Color Flashcards

Colors
  • Age: 1 to 4 years
  • Description: Common colors around us.
  • Flashcard words:Black, Blue, Green, Orange, Pink, Purple, Red, While, Yellow
