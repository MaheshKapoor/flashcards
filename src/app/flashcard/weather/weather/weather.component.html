Kids enjoy all the weather. In rainy weather, they take a bath in a natural shower and play with rain water. In summer weather, they enjoy playing till late evening. In winter weather, they enjoy playing with snow and making snowmen. We have created weather flashcard which helps kids to know all weather conditions

Weather is the state of the atmosphere at a particular time and place. It tells us the degree to which it is hot or cold, wet or dry, clam or stromy, clever or cloudy. Climate is a long term pattern of weather in a place averaged over many years.

Weather is different in different parts of the world ( World's famous places). For example, when there is a Summer in Australia and New Zealand, its Winters in America, Canada and India. The reason for this different weather is that the earth tilts a bit while revolving around the sun. The part which is near to the sun has warm weather and the part which is far from the sun has cool weather.

Weather has a direct impact on the type of the clothes we wear. We wear warm clothes in winter to keep our body warm, cotton clothes in summer to keep our body cool, rain coats to keep our body dry.

Many industries work according to the weather such as farmers grow fruits and vegetables according to the season, Clothe industry manufacture clothes according to the weather etc.

Each country's metrological department helps to forecast the weather. This helps us to prepare for upcoming extreme conditions, like strom, heavy rain, tornadoes etc. There are four essential weather instruments in every weather station like thermometer, barometer, hydrometer, rain gauge.
