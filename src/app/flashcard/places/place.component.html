Places Flashcard


Parents should show the place flashcard to the child and make the sound in front of them. We should encourage kids to mimic the sound and recognize the place.

Parents should let child recognize the places before telling them. Sometime, they may recognize incorrectly but with regular practise they will improve.

Continuous practise of the flashcard may help to develop speech or improve speech in children.

Places Flashcards

Places
  • Age: 1 to 4 years
  • Description: Popular places around us
  • Flashcard words: Airport, amusement park, Bakery, Bank, Bookstore, Bus stop, Cafe, Church, Cinema, Clothing store, Factory, Farm, Fire station, Gas station, Hospital, Hotel, Library, Museum, Park, Police station, Restaurant, School, Supermarket, Swimming pool, Theater, Train station, Zoo
