Emotions Flashcard


Parents should show the emotion flashcard to the child and make the sound in front of them. We should encourage kids to mimic the sound and recognize the emotion.

Parents should let child recognize the Emotions before telling them. Sometime, they may recognize incorrectly but with regular practise they will improve.

Continuous practise of the flashcard may help to develop speech or improve speech in children.

Emotion Flashcards

Emotions
  • Age: 1 to 4 years
  • Description: Common emotions of human beings
  • Flashcard words:Excited, Happy, Hot, Nervous, OK, Sad, Scared, Sick, Tired
Emoji Emotions
  • Age: 1 to 4 years
  • Description: Common emoji emotions used on devices
  • Flashcard words:Excited, Happy, Hot, Nervous, OK, Sad, Scared, Sick, Tired
