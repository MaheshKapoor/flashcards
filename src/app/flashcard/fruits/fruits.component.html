Fruits Flashcard


Fruits help kids to grow faster. Kids enjoy yummy fruits and its flashcard. Parents should show the fruit flashcard to the child and make the sound in front of them. We should encourage kids to mimic the sound and recognize the fruits.

Parents should let child recognize the fruits before telling them. Sometime, they may recognize incorrectly but with regular practise they will improve.

Continuous practise of the flashcard may help to develop speech or improve speech in children.

Fruit Flashcards

Fruits
  • Age: 1 to 4 years
  • Description: Commonly used fruits at home
  • Flashcard words: Apple, Apricot, Avacado, Banana, Cherry, Coconut, Custardapple, Grapes, Guava, Kiwi, Lime, Mango, Orange, Papaya, Peach, Pear, Pineapple, Plum, Pomegranate, Strawberry, Watermelon
