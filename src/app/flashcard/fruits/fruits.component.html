Fruits Flashcard


Nature has blessed us with many delicious fruits. Fruits are sweet and juicy in taste. Its available in different shapes, colors and taste. Fruits in moderate amounts are considered good for health. We have covered a variety of fruits in a fruit flashcard.

Kids like fruits from an early age. They enjoy eating fruits like apples, bananas, mango berries etc in solid or pulp form. Nowadays many baby products come in fruit flavour to attract parents.

Fruits can be used to teach different shapes like round (orange), oval (grapes) and colors like apple (red), pear (green), banana (yellow). We can use fruits to teach counting, we have counting flashcards for kids.

Fruits can be broadly classified as below:

  1. Berries: Barriers are small in size but very juicy in taste. It is perishable so we need to consume it in a few days. Few examples of barriers are blueberries, raspberries, strawberries.
  2. Pits: Pits are soft, fleshy and have seeds inside. Few examples of pits are peaches, apricots and plums.
  3. Core: Core fruits are comparatively bigger than pits. It has a thick layer of flesh containing seeds inside. Few examples of pits are apples, pears and mango.
  4. Citrus Fruits: Citrus fruits are sour in taste. It typically have thick outer skin with few seeds inside. Few examples of pits are oranges, lemons and mango
  5. Melons: Melons are juicy fruits with many seeds inside. It is large in size with thick outer skin. Few examples of melons are watermelon and honeydew.
  6. Tropical Fruits: Tropical fruits are favourite of all and are grown in warm weather. Example of tropical fruits are pineapples, pomegranates, guavas, mangoes

Different fruits are grown in different weather conditions. Apples need cold climates and mangoes need warm weather. Fruits are transported from one place to another to meet the everybodies demand.

In recent days, the food industry has evolved. They have shared so many new recipes which attracts people to consume more fruits and a healthy diet. We can use innovative home appliances to prepare healthy food at home like banana bread, mango shake, fruit salads etc.
