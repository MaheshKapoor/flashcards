Vegetables Flashcard


Vegetables are important sources of many nutrients which help kid's growth. Parents should show the vegetable flashcard to the child and make the sound in front of them. We should encourage kids to mimic the sound and recognize the vegetable.

Parents should let child recognize the vegetables before telling them. Sometime, they may recognize incorrectly but with regular practise they will improve.

Continuous practise of the flashcard may help to develop speech or improve speech in children.

Vegetable Flashcards

Vegetables
  • Age: 1 to 4 years
  • Description: Commonly used vegetable at home
  • Flashcard words: Beans, Beetroot, Broccoli, Capsicum, Cauliflower, Celery, Chilli, Cucumber, Eggplant, Garlic, Ginger, Lettuce, Mashroom, Okra, Onion, Potato, Pumpkin, Spinach, Tomato
