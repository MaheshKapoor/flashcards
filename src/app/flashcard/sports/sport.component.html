Sports Flashcard


Parents should show the Sport flashcard to the child and make the sound in front of them. We should encourage kids to mimic the sound and recognize the Sport.

Parents should let child recognize the Sports before telling them. Sometime, they may recognize incorrectly but with regular practise they will improve.

Continuous practise of the flashcard may help to develop speech or improve speech in children.

Sport Flashcards

Sports
  • Age: 1 to 4 years
  • Description: Popular Sports around us
  • Flashcard words: Badminton, Baseball, Basketball, Cycling, Football, Golf, Gymnastic, Ice skating, Running, Scuba diving, Skiing, Skateboarding, Swimming, Table Tennis, Tennis, Volleyball, windsurfing
