Gestures Flashcard


Parents should show the gesture flashcard to the child and make the sound in front of them. We should encourage kids to mimic the sound and recognize the gestures.

Parents should let child recognize the gestures before telling them. Sometime, they may recognize incorrectly but with regular practise they will improve.

Continuous practise of the flashcard may help to develop speech or improve speech in children.

Gesture Flashcards

Gestures
  • Age: 1 to 4 years
  • Description: Common Gestures in daily life
  • Flashcard words:Angry, Call me, Don't know, Follow me, Good luck, Great job, Hello, High five, Hmmm, I have an idea, I love you, I am bored, I am scared, Look, Loud, Me, Nice meeting you, No, Oh no!, Perfect, Please, Shhh!, Stay away, Stop, Well done, Yes
