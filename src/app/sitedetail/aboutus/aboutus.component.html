About Us

We created this version recently for our own kid, who was facing speech delay during COVID-19 pandemic. Kids weren’t socializing much during lockdown which affected their natural process of speech development. We created these flashcards to help our kid to know things around us.

“Omishaan.com” is a product for all parents who are facing any type of speech disorder in their kids. Regular practice of these flashcards help to overcome some speech delays. We are working on the problems faced by us personally and bring a solution for the masses. We don’t charge anything from any parents. It's free to use by anyone.

We are open for suggestions and can work on the other parenthood problems which help other parents. Please write an email to us with your suggestion at contact@omishaan.com

We love to hear about us and the feedback.
